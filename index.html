Privacy Policy

PSC POINT - Your trusted partner for competitive exam preparation

PSC POINT built this app as a free educational application to help users prepare for Kerala PSC and other competitive exams. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

The app connects to the internet only for the following purposes:

  • To receive notifications through Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM).
  • To fetch and display notification data stored in Firebase Firestore.
  • To allow users to reply to specific notifications (saved anonymously in Firestore).

Optional Permissions

The app may request certain permissions for optional features. These are not mandatory for basic use:

  • Camera: Used only when the user chooses to update their profile photo.
  • Storage / Media Access: Used to save and load profile images or other local content.
  • Vibration, Wake Lock, Notifications: Used to deliver Firebase notifications effectively.
  • Internet Access: Required to connect to Firebase services.

Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you indirectly. Below are links to their privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google)
  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (for ads, when added)

Data Security

PSC POINT does not collect personal data. All anonymous replies and notification data are securely stored in Firebase Firestore under Google's security standards.

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically for any changes.
