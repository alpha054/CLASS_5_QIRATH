Privacy Policy

PSC POINT - Your trusted partner for competitive exam preparation

PSC POINT is committed to protecting your privacy. We are proud to state that we do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users.

Information Collection

We Collect Nothing. PSC POINT operates on a strict no-data-collection policy:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage data is tracked
  • No device information is gathered
  • No location data is accessed
  • No contacts or files are accessed

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Quiz scores and progress
  • App settings and preferences
  • Bookmarked questions
  • Selected avatar (stored locally)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Internet Connectivity

The app requires internet access only for:

  • Loading quiz questions and content updates
  • Fetching current affairs (when available)

Even with internet access, no user data is transmitted from your device.

Permissions

The app requests minimal permissions for enhanced functionality:

  • Vibration: For haptic feedback on wrong answers (optional)
  • Notifications: For daily study reminders (optional)
  • Wake Lock: To keep screen active during quizzes

All permissions are optional and the app functions fully without them.

Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there's no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information. Your study progress and preferences remain securely on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app is completely safe for users of all ages, including children under 13, as we do not collect any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this policy that involve data collection, we will:

  • Clearly notify all users
  • Explain what data is collected and why
  • Provide opt-out options
  • Update this privacy policy page
